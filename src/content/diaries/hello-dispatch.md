Most of what runs here is in Bangla, but the occasional note will be in English —
and the design has to hold up in both registers. This post exists mainly to test
the Latin path: Cutive Mono for the body, Special Elite for the headline, and a
drop cap opening this very paragraph.

Newspapers earned their authority partly through restraint: a fixed palette, firm
rules, and type that stays out of the way. That is the whole ambition here — get
out of the way of the words.

If this reads cleanly beside a Bengali dispatch, the type system is doing its job.
